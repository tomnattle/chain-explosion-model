## Scope statement / 范围声明
[EN]
This manuscript reports a **joint in-silico audit** over four toy benchmark curves (laser threshold, semiconductor edge, MRI Larmor linearity, atomic-clock line shape) using one shared medium triple \((\mu,\rho,\eta)\). It does **not** claim hardware-level replication and does **not** claim first-principles derivation of dispersion exponents.
